Welcome


Today when computers and data have become the

lifeline of any organization, we cyber yodhas are here

to elucidate about cyber security and take a step

towards making a cyber secure world. Online

activities are vulnerable to crime and can

compromise personal safety as effectively as

everyday crimes. The only difference between a

normal crime and a cybercrime lies in the tool used

to commit the crime. Cybercrime can be categorized

into two categories - (a) The Computer as a Target

to attack other computers which are inclusive of

crimes like phishing and ransomware (b) The

Computer as a weapon to commit real-world crimes

which include crimes like cyber-harassment and

financial fraud. Our cyber shatrus a.k.a hackers

have different motives like Greed, Power, Publicity,

Revenge, Adventure, Desire to access forbidden

information, Destructive mindset, etc. Cyber shatrus

can destroy important data and also cause

defamation leading to a negative impact on society

either directly or indirectly. Let's fight the cyber

shatrus with rationality and become cyber yodhas!
